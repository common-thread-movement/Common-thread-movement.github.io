Essays

Below are summaries of the major essays. Full versions are available on request.

Bless G-d, Bless America, Bless Wherever You're From

A long-form civic-nationalist essay exploring unity, conscience, responsibility, moral rhythm, and why a nation’s strength comes from voluntary duty rather than dominance.

The Moral Rhythm

An explanation of how societies thrive when individuals live in harmony with conscience, truth, and responsibility — and how ignoring that rhythm leads to civic decay.

Duty Over Dominance

A critique of tribalism and partisan identity as false sources of strength, and a call to stand beyond group pressure in service to the common good.
